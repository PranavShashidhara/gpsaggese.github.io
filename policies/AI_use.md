- We don't explicitly prohibit to use AI for coding, writing, and so on

- We require to use it responsibly

1. You must be able to explain everything in your project
   - Every single line of code, every single comment, etc.
   - If you are not able to do that, you are using the AI improperly

2. Results with help of AI should be of higher quality than results without

3. Be careful from becoming dependent of AI
   - If without AI, you can't work (e.g., write code,

4. Use AI for brainstorming, but always be critical and exercise your own
   judgement against hallucinations and having the machine take over

5. Avoid AI artifacts
   - Humans are put down by content that looks AI-generated
   - Avoid obvious signs of it such as:
     - Infamous emdashes
     - Page separators
     - Excessive markdown formatting (bold, italic)
     - ...
   - I know you are thinking I'm going to use GP's `/text.humanize` to fix
     that... yes I know
